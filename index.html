1st Question

What is XPBoost and how does it work?

XPBoost is an innovative learning platform designed to help individuals enhance their skills through structured roadmaps and modules. It offers a variety of courses in development, design, and languages, providing users with a clear path to achieve their learning goals.

2nd Question

Is XPBoost open source?

Yes, XPBoost is open source. The project is publicly available and open for learning, adaptation, and collaboration.
